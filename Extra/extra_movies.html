Movies Featuring Science

In this section, I recommend outstanding movies featuring mathematicians, physicists, and scientific discovery. I tried to organize the list by category. For each movie, you will also find a direct link to its IMDb page for more information. Movies within each category are ordered chronologically by release year.

Space & Physics

2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

Synopsis: A visually stunning journey through space and time, exploring human evolution, artificial intelligence, and extraterrestrial life.

Director: Stanley Kubrick
Main Cast: Keir Dullea, Gary Lockwood

Apollo 13 (1995)

Synopsis: The harrowing true story of the Apollo 13 mission and the astronauts' struggle to return safely to Earth after an onboard explosion.

Director: Ron Howard
Main Cast: Tom Hanks, Kevin Bacon, Bill Paxton

Contact (1997)

Synopsis: A scientist discovers evidence of extraterrestrial life and embarks on a journey that challenges science and faith.

Director: Robert Zemeckis
Main Cast: Jodie Foster, Matthew McConaughey

October Sky (1999)

Synopsis: The true story of a coal miner's son inspired by the launch of Sputnik to pursue rocketry and science.

Director: Joe Johnston
Main Cast: Jake Gyllenhaal, Chris Cooper, Laura Dern

Gravity (2013)

Synopsis: Two astronauts struggle to survive after their shuttle is destroyed in orbit.

Director: Alfonso Cuarón
Main Cast: Sandra Bullock, George Clooney

Gagarin: First in Space (2013)

Synopsis: The story of Yuri Gagarin, the first human to journey into outer space aboard Vostok 1 in 1961.

Director: Pavel Parkhomenko
Main Cast: Yaroslav Zhalnin, Mikhail Filippov, Olga Ivanova

Interstellar (2014)

Synopsis: Astronauts travel through a wormhole seeking a new home for humanity.

Director: Christopher Nolan
Main Cast: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

The Theory of Everything (2014)

Synopsis: The life and achievements of physicist Stephen Hawking.

Director: James Marsh
Main Cast: Eddie Redmayne, Felicity Jones

The Martian (2015)

Synopsis: An astronaut stranded on Mars uses science and ingenuity to survive.

Director: Ridley Scott
Main Cast: Matt Damon, Jessica Chastain, Kristen Wiig

Hidden Figures (2016)

Synopsis: The untold story of three brilliant African-American women mathematicians at NASA who helped launch John Glenn into orbit.

Director: Theodore Melfi
Main Cast: Taraji P. Henson, Octavia Spencer, Janelle Monáe

First Man (2018)

Synopsis: A biographical drama about Neil Armstrong and the events leading up to the Apollo 11 moon landing.

Director: Damien Chazelle
Main Cast: Ryan Gosling, Claire Foy

The Spacewalker (2017)

Synopsis: The true story of cosmonaut Alexei Leonov, who became the first human to conduct a spacewalk during the Voskhod 2 mission in 1965.

Director: Dmitriy Kiselev
Main Cast: Evgeniy Mironov, Konstantin Khabenskiy, Vladimir Ilin

Sputnik (2020)

Synopsis: In 1983, a Soviet cosmonaut returns to Earth with a mysterious and deadly alien creature inside him.

Director: Egor Abramenko
Main Cast: Oksana Akinshina, Pyotr Fyodorov, Fyodor Bondarchuk

Don't Look Up (2021)

Synopsis: Two astronomers try to warn the world about a comet heading for Earth.

Director: Adam McKay
Main Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Jennifer Lawrence, Meryl Streep

The Challenge (2023)

Synopsis: A Russian drama filmed partly aboard the International Space Station, where a surgeon is sent to space to save a cosmonaut’s life.

Director: Klim Shipenko
Main Cast: Yuliya Peresild, Miloš Biković, Vladimir Mashkov

Oppenheimer (2023)

Synopsis: The story of J. Robert Oppenheimer and the creation of the atomic bomb.

Director: Christopher Nolan
Main Cast: Cillian Murphy, Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr.

Mathematics & Genius

Good Will Hunting (1997)

Synopsis: A janitor at MIT reveals his genius for mathematics.

Director: Gus Van Sant
Main Cast: Matt Damon, Robin Williams, Ben Affleck

A Beautiful Mind (2001)

Synopsis: The life and struggles of mathematician John Nash.

Director: Ron Howard
Main Cast: Russell Crowe, Jennifer Connelly, Ed Harris

21 (2008)

Synopsis: MIT students use math and card counting to win in Las Vegas.

Director: Robert Luketic
Main Cast: Jim Sturgess, Kevin Spacey, Kate Bosworth

X+Y (2014)

Synopsis: A teenage math prodigy competes in the International Mathematical Olympiad.

Director: Morgan Matthews
Main Cast: Asa Butterfield, Rafe Spall, Sally Hawkins

The Imitation Game (2014)

Synopsis: The story of Alan Turing and his team cracking the Enigma code during World War II.

Director: Morten Tyldum
Main Cast: Benedict Cumberbatch, Keira Knightley, Matthew Goode

The Man Who Knew Infinity (2015)

Synopsis: The journey of Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan at Cambridge.

Director: Matt Brown
Main Cast: Dev Patel, Jeremy Irons

Hidden Figures (2016)

Synopsis: The untold story of three brilliant African-American women mathematicians at NASA who helped launch John Glenn into orbit.

Director: Theodore Melfi
Main Cast: Taraji P. Henson, Octavia Spencer, Janelle Monáe

Finance & Economics

Goodfellas (1990)

Synopsis: The story of Henry Hill and his life in the mob, covering his rise and fall in the Mafia.

Director: Martin Scorsese
Main Cast: Ray Liotta, Robert De Niro, Joe Pesci

The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)

Synopsis: The rise and fall of stockbroker Jordan Belfort, who built a fortune through crime and corruption.

Director: Martin Scorsese
Main Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Jonah Hill, Margot Robbie

The Big Short (2015)

Synopsis: A group of investors predict and profit from the 2008 financial crisis using data analysis and unconventional thinking.

Director: Adam McKay
Main Cast: Christian Bale, Steve Carell, Ryan Gosling, Brad Pitt

Science, Technology & Society

Brothers (2009)

Synopsis: A soldier presumed dead returns home, causing emotional turmoil for his family.

Director: Jim Sheridan
Main Cast: Tobey Maguire, Jake Gyllenhaal, Natalie Portman

Salt (2010)

Synopsis: A CIA agent goes on the run after being accused of being a Russian spy.

Director: Phillip Noyce
Main Cast: Angelina Jolie, Liev Schreiber, Chiwetel Ejiofor

Limitless (2011)

Synopsis: A struggling writer discovers a mysterious drug that unlocks his brain's full potential.

Director: Neil Burger
Main Cast: Bradley Cooper, Robert De Niro, Abbie Cornish

The Town (2010)

Synopsis: A group of Boston bank robbers plan their biggest heist while one of them falls for a hostage.

Director: Ben Affleck
Main Cast: Ben Affleck, Rebecca Hall, Jon Hamm, Jeremy Renner

Snowden (2016)

Synopsis: The story of Edward Snowden and his decision to leak classified NSA documents.

Director: Oliver Stone
Main Cast: Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Shailene Woodley, Melissa Leo
