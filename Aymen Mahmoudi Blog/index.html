Welcome to my personal blog

Welcome to my personal blog! Here, I share knowledge, tutorials, and insights on a variety of topics in science, technology, and creative tools. Whether you're a student, researcher, or enthusiast, you'll find resources to help you learn, create, and explore.

About Me

My name is Aymen Mahmoudi, and I am a researcher in the field of 2D materials. I have a passion for sharing knowledge and helping others discover the fascinating world of science and technology. This blog is a platform for me to share my expertise and connect with like-minded individuals.

I have a background in physics and materials science, with a focus on two-dimensional materials like graphene and transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs). My goal is to make complex topics accessible and engaging for everyone.

In addition to my research, I enjoy exploring various computer skills, photography, and music production. This blog reflects my diverse interests and aims to provide valuable content across these areas.

Whether you're looking to learn about 2D materials, improve your computer skills, enhance your photography, or dive into music production, you'll find something of interest here. I believe in the power of knowledge sharing and community building, and I hope this blog serves as a resource for you.

Feel free to explore the different sections, leave comments, and share your thoughts. I'm excited to embark on this journey with you and look forward to your feedback and contributions.

What You'll Find on This Blog

  • 2D Materials: In-depth articles on graphene, transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs), and the latest research in the field of two-dimensional materials.
  • Computer Skills: Practical guides on Linux, LaTeX, GIMP, Inkscape, Zotero, screen recording, and more to boost your productivity and creativity.
  • Photography: Tutorials and tips on camera sensors, lenses, exposure, and post-processing to help you capture and enhance stunning images.
  • Music Production: Insights into music software, distribution, mixing, and mastering for aspiring musicians and producers.
  • Extra: A collection of inspiring quotes, movie recommendations, riddles, and stories about remarkable people.

How to Use This Blog

  • Browse the menu bar and side bar to navigate between categories and topics.
  • Use the tools box for quick access to recommended software and resources.
  • Each section is designed to be beginner-friendly, with step-by-step guides and practical examples.
